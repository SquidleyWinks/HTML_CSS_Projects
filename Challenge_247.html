Article Element

The article element does not render any effects on its own, but can be styled with CSS.
It is meant to indicate a complete unit of information, one that can be distributed independently of the rest of the site.

Details and Summary Tags

The details tag is used to create a widget that can expand to show additional information -- the Summary tag is used to dispaly a visual heading for the additional info.
